General

What is LaughTrak?
LaughTrak is a native iOS app for stand-up comedy fans. It lets you follow your favorite comedians, browse their specials and albums, discover nearby comedy clubs on a map, find live shows in your area, and receive notifications when followed comedians release new material or perform near you.
Is LaughTrak free?
Yes — LaughTrak is free to download and use. The app includes an optional Tip Jar (in-app purchase) if you'd like to support development, but all core features are available at no cost.
Do I need to create an account?
No account needed. LaughTrak uses your existing Apple ID and iCloud to sync your followed comedians across your devices — nothing extra to sign up for.
Which devices does LaughTrak support?
LaughTrak requires iOS 18 or later and runs on iPhone. iCloud sync works across any of your Apple devices signed in to the same Apple ID.

Comedians & Content

How do I follow a comedian?
Tap the search bar on the Comedians tab, type the comedian's name, and tap on their profile in the results. From their detail page, tap the Follow button to add them to your lineup.
Where does comedian data come from?
Comedian profiles, specials, and albums are sourced from a dedicated comedy database. Additional special metadata may come from TMDB (The Movie Database).
A comedian I follow is missing or their data looks wrong. What should I do?
Comedian data is pulled from third-party databases. If something looks incorrect, please contact us with the comedian's name and what appears to be wrong — we'll look into it.
How do I unfollow a comedian?
On the Comedians tab, swipe left on a comedian's card, or tap into their profile and use the Unfollow option. You can also unfollow all comedians at once from Settings → Unfollow All.

Map & Live Shows

How does the comedy club map work?
The Map tab uses your device's location (with your permission) to show comedy clubs near you on an interactive map. Tap any pin to see the club's name, address, rating, and contact information.
How are nearby shows found?
Live comedy events are powered by Ticketmaster. LaughTrak sends your location to Ticketmaster to retrieve upcoming shows near you. Tap any event to get ticketing links.
Why isn't my location loading?
Make sure you've granted LaughTrak location access. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → LaughTrak and set it to "While Using the App."

Notifications

How do I turn on notifications?
In the app, go to the Settings tab → Notifications. Toggle on "New Video Releases" and/or "Tour Notifications." If you haven't already granted notification permission, iOS will prompt you to do so.
How does LaughTrak know when something new is released?
LaughTrak periodically checks the Comedy database in the background and compares current specials against what it last saw. If something new appears for a comedian you follow, you'll get a local notification.
How do I set the search radius for tour notifications?
Go to Settings → Location → Search Radius and choose your preferred distance. Tour notifications will only fire when a followed comedian has a show within that radius.

iCloud & Data

Does LaughTrak sync my data across devices?
Yes. Your list of followed comedians is stored in your iCloud private database and syncs across all your Apple devices signed in to the same Apple ID — automatically, with no setup required.
How do I delete all my data?
Go to Settings → Advanced → Erase iCloud Data to remove all synced data from iCloud. Then use Reset All Settings to clear local storage. Finally, delete the app from your device.
Does LaughTrak collect personal data?
No. LaughTrak does not collect or store personal data on our servers. Your data lives on your device and in your personal iCloud account. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
