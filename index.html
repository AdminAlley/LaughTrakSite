Everything you need

Built for stand-up fans.

LaughTrak brings everything about your favorite comedians into one beautifully designed iOS app.

🎤

Follow Comedians

Build your personal roster of favorite comedians. LaughTrak keeps your list in sync across all your Apple devices via iCloud.

🎬

Browse Specials & Albums

Instantly see every special and comedy album from the comedians you follow. Never lose track of their catalog again.

🗺️

Comedy Club Map

Discover comedy clubs near you on an interactive map. Tap any club to see details, ratings, and contact info — then go laugh.

🎟️

Live Shows Near You

Find out when comedians you follow are performing near you. Powered by Ticketmaster, with links to buy tickets right from the app.

🔔

New Release Alerts

Get notified the moment a comedian you follow drops a new special or album. Be first in line, every time.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Your followed comedians live in iCloud — switch between your iPhone, iPad, or a new phone and everything is right where you left it.

🎙️

Getting started

Up and running in seconds.

No account required. Just install, follow a few comedians, and the app does the rest.

01

Search for Comedians

Type any comedian's name into the search bar. LaughTrak pulls from a rich comedy database to find who you're looking for.

02

Tap to Follow

Tap a comedian to see their profile, specials, and tour dates — then follow them with a single tap to add them to your lineup.

03

Explore Their Work

Browse their full catalog of specials and albums. Tap a special to find where to watch it.

04

Find Shows Near You

Open the Map tab to discover comedy clubs nearby, or check the live events list when a comedian you follow comes to town.

Stay in the loop

Never miss a moment.

LaughTrak quietly watches for you. The moment something happens with a comedian you follow, you'll know about it — right on your lock screen.

  • ★ New release alerts — instant notification when a followed comedian drops new material
  • ★ Nearby tour alerts — get notified when a followed comedian is performing within your search radius
  • ★ Fully configurable — toggle each notification type individually in Settings
  • ★ Custom search radius — set how far "nearby" means to you
